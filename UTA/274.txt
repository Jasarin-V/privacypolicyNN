Cookies
EDC websites uses cookie technology. A cookie is a small file that contains information sent by a website that is saved on your hard disk by your computer's browser. Cookies store information that a website may need to personalize your experience and to gather website statistical data, such as which pages you visit, what material you download, your internet provider's domain name and country, and the addresses of websites you visited immediately before, and after, visiting a particular website.

EDC websites use cookies to provide a unique identifier to your computer so that we can generate statistics regarding usage of EDC websites, such as the percentage of repeat visitors and other general statistics. The unique identifiers are not matched with any personal information. Moreover, cookies used by EDC websites do not store any Personal Information about you.

To browse the Internet and EDC websites without cookies, you can configure your browser to reject all cookies or to notify you when each cookie is offered so that you can accept or reject each cookie. You may not be able to conduct some transactions or use some services on EDC websites if you disable your browser from accepting cookies.
